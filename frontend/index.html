How HungerConnect Works

Our three-step process makes it simple to connect food with those who need it most.

1

Post

Food donors post available food or volunteers report locations of people in need

2

Match

Our system automatically matches nearby needs with available food

3

Deliver

Volunteers deliver the food and confirm completion
